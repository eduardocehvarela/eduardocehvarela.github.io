Research interests

My general research areas are Applied Machine Learning, Data Mining, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Agentic AI. In particular, my Ph.D. research was in the area of Recommender Systems. I have worked on exploring Unsupervised and Deep Learning techniques and analyzing textual reviews to provide better recommendation strategies.
